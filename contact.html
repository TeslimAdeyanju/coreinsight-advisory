Frequently asked questions

What size of business do you work with?

We primarily work with small and medium enterprises in the United Kingdom, typically with annual revenues between £2 million and £50 million. These are businesses that need better financial insight and automation but may not have in-house analytics capability.

How much does it cost?

Every project is different. After our initial conversation, we provide a clear proposal with fixed pricing for defined deliverables. There are no hidden costs or open-ended billing.

How long do projects take?

It depends on the scope. A simple dashboard might take two to four weeks. A comprehensive reporting and forecasting solution might take two to three months. We agree timelines upfront.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. Most of our work is delivered remotely, which keeps costs down and makes scheduling easier. We use video calls, screen sharing and collaborative tools. We can visit on-site when needed.
