We work with small and growing UK businesses who've outgrown spreadsheets but don't yet have an in-house analytics team. These challenges usually come with the territory.

Slow month-end reporting

Limited visibility

You lack clear insight into profit margins, cash flow drivers and key performance metrics across the business.
